Business Valuation Outreach


      Haas Business Valuation Services is involved in a variety of educational outreach activities.  These include numerous speeches sponsored by such professional organizations as the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA), the Pennsylvania Institute of Certified Public Accountants (PICPA), estate planning councils in Delaware, West Virginia and Pennsylvania, various Pennsylvania county bar associations, and the Pennsylvania Economic Association.

      In addition, R. Victor Haas, Jr. has co-authored a course on business valuation, used by the PICPA and other state accounting societies in professional continuing education programs, and has co-designed business valuation software in conjunction with a nationally-known estate planning attorney.  Mr. Haas has published articles on topics relevant to the field in professional, academic and trade publications.  In 1993, he contributed two chapters on business valuation topics to Wiley Law Publication's Valuation Strategies in Divorce, Volume 3.
