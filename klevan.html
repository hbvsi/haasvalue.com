Professional Qualifications


Sandra R. Klevan, CPA

PRESENT POSITION:
Sandra R. Klevan is a Senior Business Analyst at Haas Business Valuation Services Inc. She is responsible for industry research, financial analysis, economic analysis, and valuation of privately-held businesses as well as the calculation of damages.  The appraisals are used for numerous purposes, including estate and gift tax purposes, marital dissolution, ESOPs, dissenting shareholder litigation, and in an advisory capacity to buyers and sellers.  Damage calculations are done on behalf of both plaintiffs and defendants in contract and tort claims.  When requested, Ms. Klevan provides expert testimony to support her opinions.  She is also involved in other accounting service work provided by the firm.

EDUCATION:
Ms. Klevan has a Master in Business Administration degree from St. Joseph's University with a concentration in finance.  She also has a Bachelor of Science in Accounting degree from St. Joseph's.

EXPERIENCE:
From 1991 to 1995, Ms. Klevan was employed by a Philadelphia-area business valuation firm as a Business Analyst.  In that capacity, she had essentially the same responsibilities as in her present position.

Prior to that, Ms. Klevan was employed as a Certified Public Accountant with Price Waterhouse for five years.

ASSOCIATION MEMBERSHIPS:
Member, American Institute of Certified Public Accountants; Member, Pennsylvania Institute of Certified Public Accountants; Member, American Society of Appraisers.

LECTURES/PUBLICATIONS:
Ms. Klevan has taught various finance courses as an adjunct professor at accredited business schools in the area.  She has also lectured on the topic of business valuation at seminars for accounting professionals and attorneys.  Articles on business valuation and related topics co-authored by Ms. Klevan have appeared in professional and trade publications.
